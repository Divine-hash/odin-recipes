Jollof Rice

Procedure For Cooking Jollof Rice

The jollof rice recipe discussed here is a Nigerian recipe. For years there has been a serious debate between Nigeria and Ghana about who cooks the best Jollof rice, but I believe it depends on the experience of the cook preparing the dish.

Ingridients

  • 3¾ cups (750g) long grain parboiled rice
  • 2-3 cups of Tomato stew
  • chicken
  • 2 medium onions
  • 3 knorr cubes(spice made in Nigeria)
  • 2 teaspoons of thyme
  • 2 teaspoons of Nigerian curry poweder

Steps

  1. Prepare the tomato stew.
  2. Cook the chicken with thyme, knorr cubes, and 2 chopped bulbs of onions. When done, grill it in an oven or fry it.
  3. Parboil the rice and put in a sieve to drain.
  4. Pour the chicken stock and tomato stew into a pot and leave to boil.
  5. Add the drained parboiled rice, curry powder, salt and pepper to taste. Add water and ensure the water level is the same level of the rice.
  6. Cover the pot and leave to cook on low medium to heat.
  7. Cook till it is done. If the rice was parboiled, it should be ready once the water has dried.
